Purple Flowers 

     Purple flowers come in every shape and size, from tiny grape hyacinths to 
towering alliums and climbing clematis, But you just can't beat purple pansies 
for their mood-bossing pep of purple, often with an eye-catching yellow center. 
Fill a pot with these striking purple beauties, the perfectspring plants, and 
they'll bring a smile to any face that passes them by.
